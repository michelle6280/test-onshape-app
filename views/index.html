Cube with A Hole

Goals:

This page will teach you how to sketch and extrude a cube with a hole in the middle. You will be introduced to some of the basic functionalities: "Sketch" and "Extrude." You will also be shown how to rotate and orient your CAD model to see different perspectives and plane views.

Sketch a 2" x 2" square

Start out with a new Part Studio Sketch, which you can add by clicking on the "+" button located at the bottom left hand corner of the screen. At the origin, where the black circle is located, draw a Center Point Rectangle by going to the tool bar on the top and then clicking on the icon with a rectangle.
