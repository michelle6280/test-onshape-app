Don't know where to start? Start by letting us help you:


"WHAT'S CAD/ONSHAPE?"

Complete beginner? Welcome! We recommend that you start out with familiarizing yourself with Onshape's platform because even if you've tried out different CAD platforms, every one is different. Go out our Tools page to get used to the Onshape environment: no CAD experience needed! However, this is where you can start to familiarize yourself with some of the tools that Onshape provides. Maybe then take a look at the Tools tab on the Resources page to learn how to use each of the tools.

"I'VE DABBLED IN CAD A BIT."

So you've been exposed to CAD and even Onshape already, but still a little rough on the skills? Start off with a challenge or two on our Activities page to assess just how comfortable you really are! Start out with the harder ones if you're really feeling confident, but otherwise, start with creating a cube with a hole. Practicing is where most of the learning happens, so try it for yourself and go through some of our approaches as well. Refer to the FAQs tab on the Resources page if you have any questions.

"CAD IS MY OTHER HALF. WHAT'S NEXT?"

Alright you expert, wondering what this app could possibly do for you? Well we can still point you to our Activities page: start off with the more difficult challenges and try to solve them in at least 3 different ways! Still want more? Then go through our gallery of different CAD projects and try to recreate them (and we won't be giving you any guidelines or instructions on where to start, so good luck!). Go off and CAD away!
