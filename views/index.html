Cube with A Hole

Goals:

This page will teach you how to sketch and extrude a cube with a hole in the middle. You will be introduced to Sketches (on a plane and face) along with Add/Remove Extrude. You will also be shown how to rotate and orient your CAD model to see different perspectives and plane views.

Sketch a 2" x 2" square

Start out with a new Part Studio Sketch, which you can add by clicking on the "+" button located at the bottom left corner of the screen.

At the origin, draw a Center Point Rectangle by going to the tool bar on the top and then clicking on the icon with a rectangle.

Then click once on the origin, move your mouse outward, and click again to stop the sketch. ** Note: Every time you draw a new sketch, Onshape prompts you to define plane to draw the sketch on. It's like picking which side of a sheet of paper you will be draw on. **

On the tool bar, we will use the Dimension tool to specify the lengths of the sides. Click once on a side of the rectangle and move your mouse outward. Click again to adjust the length to 2in.

Do the same for the other side of the rectangle to create a square.
